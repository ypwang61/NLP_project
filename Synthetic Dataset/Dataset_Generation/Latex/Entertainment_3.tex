\frametitle{The Evolution of Entertainment}
\begin{columns}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Traditional Forms of Entertainment}}
Traditional entertainment includes theater, music, dance, and sports. It was primarily imagery and people’s physical ability to perform, and the available entertainment was limited to one's geographical location.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Modern Technology in Entertainment}}
With the advent of modern technologies like television, radio, and internet, entertainment has been redefined. It provides a way for people to enjoy shows, movies, and music from all over the world from their own homes.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Future of Entertainment}}
The future of entertainment is shaped by emerging technologies like virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR) and gaming consoles. These technologies are creating immersive and interactive entertainment experiences that are breaking the geographical and temporal constraints of traditional entertainment.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\end{columns}
