\frametitle{Understanding Equality}
\begin{columns}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Concept and Importance of Equality}}
Equality refers to ensuring everyone is treated fairly and equally, regardless of their differences. It is a core principle for a just society, promoting fairness and opportunities for all individuals.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Equality vs. Equity}}
Though similar, equality and equity are distinct. Equality focuses on treating everyone the same, whereas equity involves distributing resources based on an individual’s needs.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\begin{column}{0.3\textwidth}
\begin{block}{\textbf{Challenges to Achieving Equality}}
Despite progress, numerous barriers to equality persist, including systemic discrimination, socioeconomic disparities, and stereotype bias. Working towards equality thus necessitates addressing these obstacles.
\end{block}
\end{column}
\end{columns}
